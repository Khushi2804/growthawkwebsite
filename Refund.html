Refund Policy

At Growthawk, we are proud to offer the best-in-class quality Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solutions to achieve business operational efficiency,increase productivity, and enhance overall performance. At other times, circumstances may call for a refund. For better clarity and fairness to our customers, we have clearly outlined our refund policy as follows.

1. Eligibility for Refunds
Refund Application for Growthawk's ERP Solutions
An application for refund of Growthawk's ERP solutions would be made within 30 days from the date of the purchase or subscription. For the request to be accepted, it should have a direct connection to a specific circumstance or condition; that is, if there arises technical problems, which could not be solved within a reasonable time frame or if the product does not work as stated.

2. Refund Procedure
In case you are not satisfied with our ERP solution, please raise a complaint to our customer support team at support@growthawk.com within 30 days from your date of purchase. Please do provide us with the following information to understand your request better:
* Evidence of your purchase or order number
* Description of the problem you're experiencing
* Any troubleshooting steps taken so far
Our team will consider this and, if it's applicable, then we can also provide you with a refund, whether full or partial, depending on your contract and the nature of the problem.

3. Non-Refundable Products

Of course, there are also some that are non-refundable, such as:

* Customization or bespoke ERP solution based on your business needs

* Services provided, such as setup and training

* Subscriptions that havè utilized more than 30 days are beyond the refund window

4. How to Get Approved for Refund

Once we have received your request, our processing team will examine it. A determination shall be made within 5-7 business days from when we received your request. If there is an approved refund to be paid, then that shall be issued through the ame pay method that was initially used. The refund could take some days appearing on the bank statement depending on the payment provider.

5. Modifications to Refund Policy

Growthawk can modify and update this refund policy at any time it deems necessary. Changes are posted on our website. We, at Growthawk, are committed to providing the best in class ERP solutions for your business. If you have coneerns or questions about our refund policy or our ERP products, just get in touch with our support team.
