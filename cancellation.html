Cancellation

We at Growthawk tend to be cautious at the delivery of the best-in-class ERP solution which meets your requirement. Now, we understand that, for some unknown reason, you may want to cancel your subscription with Growthawk for ERP services. To make it hassle-free and transparent to the practice, we are putting the cancellation policy on this page.

Cancellation Process:

In case you wish to unsubscribe from our ERP software, you may contact us using your customer support contact details available on our website, or you can reach directly at support@growthawk.com. We would appreciate it if you could provide your account information, including your email address with which you subscribed to our site and the kind of plan you are subscribed for so that we may assist you immediately in your request.

Terms of cancellation

Notice Period:If you cancel your subscription, there is minimum notice period of 30 days before next billing cycle, So on your next period, there won't be any charge on your bill, and your cancellation request would be processed.

Refund Policy: In our subscription model, there would be no refunds of partial months. However, all services would be available till the completion of the billing period.

Data Retention: We will retain your data for the next 30 days after cancellation. You can request your data copy within these 30 days for transfer to another system, and then, as a matter of practice, as stated in our privacy policy, we will delete your data securely.

Post-Cancellation Support As your subscription will becanceled, Growthawk's support team will still be available to you in case of any queries or issues faced by von while undereoine the transition period.
